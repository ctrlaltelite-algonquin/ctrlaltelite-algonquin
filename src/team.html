Our Team Together, we achieve more. We are a group of passionate individuals, each bringing our unique skills and perspectives to the table. Our diverse backgrounds and experiences fuel our creativity and drive us to push the boundaries of what is possible.

Dylan Crozier Designer

My name is Dylan Crozier, and I serve as the Design Lead of Project Zeus, focusing on gameplay and story. My role encompasses crafting design documentation and overseeing the implementation of design concepts.

I also act as the primary Level Designer and Environment Artist. This involves conceptualizing levels, developing layouts, and bringing them to life within Unreal Engine. I strive to balance aesthetics, functionality, and player immersion to enhance both gameplay and narrative.

My dual focus on design and art allows me to maintain a cohesive vision across Project Zeus, ensuring that story elements and gameplay mechanics align with the world we’re building. By iterating on player feedback and refining level designs, I work to deliver a polished and compelling experience that reflects the team’s creativity and dedication.
